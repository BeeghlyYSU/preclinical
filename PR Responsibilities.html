Preclinical Program Responsibilities and Requirements

The Department of Teacher Education and Leadership Studies uses licensure specific terminology in identifying teacher candidates respective programs and requirements. Mentor teachers and university supervisors may need clarification from their teacher candidate to ensure they are utilizing the correct forms for the preclinical experience.

Licensure Programs

  • AYA (Adolescent Young Adult) grades 7-12 this is a content specific license (math, science, language arts, social studies)
  • ECIS (Early Childhood Intervention Specialist) dual licensed program - grades PK-3 and grades PK-3 special education
  • ELIS (Primary/Primary Intervention Specialist) dual licensed program - grades PK-5 and grades PK-5 special education
  • MCE (Middle Childhood Education) grades 4-9 content specific license in 2 content areas and can be any combination - math, science, language arts, social studies.
  • SPED (Special Education) grades PK-12 with mild/moderate disability emphasis.
